About

We are a new team that is starting in the second semester of 2019, currently recruiting people for our line ups for each and every team, we are also waiting for the demand of players to decide which games will be played for the team. On the other hand, it is already decided teams for League of Legends, Fortnite and Counter Strike: Global Offensive. In this website will be able to get your first touch with the team and even, if you don't know anything about esports, you will be introduced to some of the games that this squad will be initially playing. The esports are a new mode of competition and it includes, of course, the teams, supporters, sponsors and nearly everything that the well known sports have. They are no different in many aspects from the real sports, besides the fact that they are not practiced out of the virtual world and for that reason they have the 'e' in esports which means eletronic. Check our line ups and the games that we are currently playing in the team page.
